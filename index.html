Visit Bournemouth

As a hot tourist destination and cultural center, as well as a town with a growing student population, there are plenty of things to do and places to go when evening turns into night in Bournemouth ...

Top three activities to do at Bournemouth

Go for a piknik or coffee in the local park

This is a small park ideal for sitting and watching people go by. Or play crazy golf. Enjoy a tea or coffee at one of the little cafes. Ideal place to go to pass an hour or two. This park links the beach area to the High Street.

Attend a airshow in the town

The UK's biggest air festival, 4-days of action and entertainment in the air, on land and at sea! Award-winning and internationally renowned, the Bournemouth Air Festival has entertained more than 8.5 million people since 2008.

Visit the Jurassic Coast World Heritage Site

185 million years in the making. Your guide to the area. Stunning Coastline. World Heritage Site. Walk, Stay, Help, Donate.
